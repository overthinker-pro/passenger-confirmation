Before You Start

Quick instructions for a smoother trip experience

These notes are meant to keep the group organized and help every participant know what to expect before, during, and after the tour.

Timing

Departure Time

Please arrive at Bethany Aramana at least 15-20 minutes before departure.

Meals

Meals & Breaks

Breakfast, lunch, and dinner breaks are planned, so please follow the group timing closely.

Families

Family Friendly

Keep children close at every stop and help them stay with the group during movement.

Checklist Guide

Helpful guidance for the full day

  • Wear comfortable clothes and footwear suitable for a full day trip.
  • Carry water bottles, basic medicines, and any personal essentials you may need.
  • Keep phones charged and, if possible, bring a power bank.
  • Travel light and avoid bringing too many bags unless necessary.
  • Please report on time so the group can leave without delay.
  • Confirm your presence with the organizers before boarding.
  • Families should gather together early to avoid last-minute confusion.
  • Listen carefully to any final updates announced before departure.
  • Please stay seated safely while the vehicle is moving.
  • Keep the bus clean and use waste bags if needed.
  • Be considerate with sound, singing, and movement so all age groups stay comfortable.
  • Inform the coordinators if anyone feels unwell during the trip.
  • Respect the spiritual, cultural, and public nature of each location.
  • Follow the announced time to return to the vehicle after each stop.
  • Children and students should remain with their family or assigned group.
  • Be mindful while taking photos and avoid blocking group movement.
  • Please be patient during meal stops and cooperate with the group flow.
  • Take care of elders and children, especially during boarding and walking.
  • Share space kindly and help maintain a calm, cheerful atmosphere.
  • Inform organizers immediately if any item is misplaced or any issue comes up.

Final Reminder

Let's make the day joyful, organized, and memorable for everyone.

A little punctuality, care, and cooperation from each participant will help the whole group enjoy the journey much more.
