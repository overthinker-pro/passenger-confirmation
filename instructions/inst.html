Tour Information

Everything you need to know for the Bethany Sunday School Tour 2026: Coastal Heritage Trail.

Key Dates

May 20th — May 22nd, 2026

Location

Coastal Heritage Trail Main Gate

Check-in

15 minutes prior to start

1. Registration Verification

Before the tour begins, all families must ensure their details are correctly captured in our database. Please visit the Member List via the admin portal or verify with a coordinator to check:

  • Correct spelling of all family member names.
  • Accurate count of students vs. adults (crucial for safety insurance).
  • Any specific dietary requirements or mobility notes.

2. The Date Selection Poll

On the main portal page, you will find a "Date Poll." While your registration is already confirmed, we use this poll to manage daily capacity limits for the trail.

Please select the date that best suits your family's schedule. Note that once a day reaches 100 people, it will be marked as "High Capacity" on our internal logs.

3. Preparation & Gear

The Coastal Heritage Trail covers roughly 2.5 miles of varied terrain (grass, wooden boardwalks, and packed sand). We recommend:

Footwear Closed-toe walking shoes or sneakers. Avoid flip-flops.
Hydration Bring at least 1L of water per person. Refill stations are limited.
Protection Sunscreen (SPF 30+) and a lightweight hat are highly recommended.

4. Arrival & Grouping

Groups will be organized alphabetically by family name. Upon arrival at the Main Gate:

  1. Locate your name on the printed "Daily Manfiest" at the check-in desk.
  2. Collect your colored group wristbands (Cyan for Group A, Lime for Group B).
  3. Meet your assigned Tour Guide near the Heritage Fountain.

Need Assistance?

If you have questions regarding logistics or need to change your registration details, please email tour-support@bethany.org or contact Noel (Admin) directly.
