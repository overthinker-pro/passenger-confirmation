Preparation & Gear

The Coastal Heritage Trail covers varied terrain, including grass, wooden boardwalks, and packed sand. We recommend:

Footwear Closed-toe walking shoes or sneakers. Avoid flip-flops.
Hydration Bring at least 1L of water per person. Refill stations are limited.
Protection Sunscreen and a lightweight hat are highly recommended.

Tour Day Checklist

Groups will be organized alphabetically by family name. Upon arrival at the Main Gate:

  1. Locate your name on the printed manifest at the check-in desk.
  2. Collect your colored group wristbands.
  3. Meet your assigned tour guide near the Heritage Fountain.
