Tour Day Instructions

Departure

Departure is at 5:20 AM. Please be present at least by 5:10 AM to ensure a smooth start.

Luggage

Space inside the traveller is limited. Kindly carry only essential items and limit luggage to one bag per person.

Water

Please carry at least 1 litre of water per person. Additional packaged water will be available in the vehicle.

Food Arrangements

To avoid delays, breakfast and lunch will be the same for everyone.

Dress Code

As our main visit is to Thiruvithamcode Arappally, please dress modestly. You may carry offerings if you wish (optional).

Padmanabhapuram Palace Visit

Stay close to your group, follow instructions, and return to the vehicle on time.

Note: Photography and videography inside the palace are limited and may require additional payment.

Safety & Valuables

Please take care of your belongings. Avoid carrying or wearing excessive jewellery.

Beach Visit

Students may carry an extra set of clothes if planning to play. Kindly help keep the vehicle clean.

Health

Carry any necessary medications. Those prone to motion sickness are advised to take precautions.

Let’s ensure we all follow these guidelines for a smooth, safe, and enjoyable trip. Thank you!
