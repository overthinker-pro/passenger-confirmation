Bethany Tour 2025

Explore the journey, spot by spot✨

Spot 1 - St. Thomas Dayara Vettickal

​St. Thomas Dayara in Vettickal is a historic monastery of the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church, established in 1125, renowned for its spiritual significance, including the interment of St. Gregorios of Parumala's relics and as the site of the first Holy Synod of the Malankara Church.

Breakfast – Annapoorna Tiffin House

Spot 2 - Hill Palace Museum Thrippunithura

​Hill Palace Museum in Thrippunithura, built in 1865, is Kerala's largest archaeological museum, originally serving as the royal residence of the Cochin Maharaja and now housing extensive collections of royal artifacts, ancient sculptures, and manuscripts.

Spot 3 - Jewish Synagogue Mattancherry

​The Paradesi Synagogue in Kochi, built in 1568, is the oldest active synagogue in the Commonwealth, renowned for its hand-painted Chinese tiles, Belgian chandeliers, and historic Torah scrolls.

Lunch – The Salt Restaurant

Spot 4 - St. George Orthodox Church Koonan Kurish

St. George Orthodox Koonan Kurish Old Syrian Church in Mattancherry, Kochi, stands on the historic site of the 1653 Coonan Cross Oath, a pivotal event in the Malankara Orthodox Syrian Church's history, and houses the holy relics of St. George.

Spot 5 - Vasco Da Gama Square

Vasco da Gama Square in Fort Kochi is a scenic waterfront promenade, known for its historical significance, Chinese fishing nets, and vibrant local culture.

Dinner – The Salt Restaurant
