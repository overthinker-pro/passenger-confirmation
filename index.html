Bethany Tour 2025

Tour Instructions

For All Participants

  • Carry enough water to stay hydrated throughout the day.
  • Bring an umbrella for sudden rain and a cap if you have one.
  • Wear comfortable, light clothing suitable for walking and a full day of travel. Avoid heavy or bulky clothes. Bring extra clothes only if necessary and keep luggage minimal.
  • Take only essential items to keep your luggage light and create more space for everyone.
  • The group will split into up to 3 smaller clusters in crowded places to stay safe and organized.
  • Everyone must be at Bethany Church on 21st May (Tour Day) by 5:00 AM.

For Students

  • Always listen carefully to the adults in your group. Do not go anywhere alone.
  • Running and playing are not allowed in crowded or busy areas. You will have time to enjoy and play in safe, designated spots.
  • Stay close to your group and be very careful everywhere you go.
  • Keep your valuables safe.

For Adults

  • Keep a close count of your group members at all times, especially when leaving a place.
  • Take good care of your assigned students and make sure they stay with the group.
  • Help students follow the rules kindly and remind them to stay safe.
  • Ensure students carry necessary medications and motion sickness tablets if needed.
